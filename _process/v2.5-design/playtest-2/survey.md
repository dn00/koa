# Post-Game Survey — Playtest 2

Complete this AFTER all 3 runs. Be honest — we need real reactions, not polite ones.

---

**QR1:** In one word, how did you feel after Run 1?

**QR2:** In one word, how did you feel after Run 3?

## Part 2: Structured Assessment (1-7 scale, 1=strongly disagree, 7=strongly agree)

### Engagement
**S1:** I wanted to keep playing after my first game.
**S2:** I found myself thinking about what I'd do differently.
**S3:** I lost track of time while playing.

### Clarity
**S4:** I understood what I needed to do to win.
**S5:** When I lost, I understood why.
**S6:** The feedback after each turn helped me make better decisions.

### Depth
**S7:** There were meaningful choices to make each turn.
**S8:** I discovered something new about the game between runs.
**S9:** I believe there are strategies I haven't tried yet.

### Frustration
**S10:** I felt frustrated by things outside my control.
**S11:** When I made a mistake, it felt like MY mistake (not the game being unfair).
**S12:** The game punished me for playing in a reasonable way.

### KOA (the investigator character)
**S13:** KOA felt like a real character, not just a game system.
**S14:** KOA's responses influenced my decisions.
**S15:** I paid attention to what KOA said.

### Achievement
**S16:** Winning felt earned, not lucky.
**S17:** I wanted to get a better badge (CLEAN, THOROUGH, FLAWLESS).
**S18:** I cared about addressing all the concerns, not just winning.

### Net Promoter Score
**S19:** On a scale of 0-10, how likely would you recommend this game to a friend?
