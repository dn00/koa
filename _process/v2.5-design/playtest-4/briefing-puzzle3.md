# The Missing Remote — How to Play

Same rules as before. New scenario, new cards.

---

## The Scenario

It's Saturday afternoon. The TV remote is gone.
Last seen on the couch at 10pm Friday.
The couch cushions have been searched. Twice.
You were the last one watching TV. KOA wants answers.

- Resistance: 16
- Scrutiny limit: 5
- Turns: 3
- Concerns to address: IDENTITY, LOCATION, ALERTNESS

---

## How to Play

Each turn, pick 1-2 cards by typing their names separated by commas.

The cards will be shown when the game starts. Pay attention to the scrutiny breakdown after the game — it shows exactly what caused your scrutiny.

Good luck.
